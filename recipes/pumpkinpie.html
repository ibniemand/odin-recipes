Pumpkin Pie

Description

This best pumpkin pie recipe came about after many years of experimentations. I've finnaly prefected what I think is the ideal formula for a rich pumpkin pie that's much less likely to crack on top. Serve garnished with whipped cream and freshly grated nutmeg.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree

  • 3 egg yolks

  • 1 large egg

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condesed milk

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

  • 1/2 teaspoon fine salt

  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly gratde nutmeg

  • 1/8 teaspoon Chinese 5-spice powder

  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).

  2. Wisk together pumpkin, egg yolks, adn egg in a large bowl until smooth. Add sweetened condesed milk, cinnamon, ginger, salt, and Chinese 5-spice powder; whisk until thoroughly combined.

  3. Line a 9-inch pie plate with pie crust; crimp edges.

  4. Pour filling into crust; lihtly tap on the counter to release air bubbles.

  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.

  6. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake until just set in the middle, 30 to 40 minutes. A praing knife inserted into de filling, 1 inch from the edge, should come out clean. Allow to cool completely before serving.
